Elden Ring: Night Regin

A dark era falls when moonlit shadows reclaim the Lands Between.

Lore Overview

The Night Regin marks the rise of ancient lunar forces long sealed beneath the Eternal City. As darkness spreads, spectral beings roam freely, and forgotten gods awaken.

Key Characters

  • Queen Lunestra – Empress of the Dark Moon
  • The Pale Watchers – Silent guardians of the abyss
  • The Duskborne – Mortals twisted by lunar corruption

Plot Summary

The Tarnished must navigate an altered world, one where night never ends and fate is rewritten under silver starlight.
